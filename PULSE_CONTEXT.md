## What We Actually Do (Concrete Example: Med Spa)

> **IMPORTANT**: Don't let the "infrastructure" language obscure what we actually do day-to-day. Here's the reality:

### For a Med Spa client, we literally:

**1. Run Google Ads Campaigns**
- Build and manage Search campaigns targeting keywords like "Botox near me," "lip filler [city]," "CoolSculpting cost"
- Optimize bidding for high-LTV procedures (not just cheap clicks)
- Write ad copy, test headlines, manage extensions
- Report on CAC per procedure, not just overall cost-per-lead

**2. Run Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Campaigns**
- Create demand-generation campaigns with before/after creatives, offer-based ads, and retargeting
- Build lookalike audiences based on their best patients
- Manage budgets, test audiences, refresh creative

**3. Execute SEO & Local Search**
- Optimize Google Business Profile for "near me" searches
- Build procedure-specific landing pages (e.g., "/morpheus8", "/prp-hair-restoration")
- Write blog content targeting long-tail keywords
- Build citations and local backlinks
- Structure content for AI/Answer Engine visibility (AEO)

**4. Manage Lead Operations**
- Set up CRM automations (speed-to-lead, follow-up sequences)
- SMS confirmations and no-show reduction
- Lead scoring to prioritize high-intent prospects

### The "Infrastructure" Difference

What makes this different from a typical agency?

- **Shared Learning**: When we find a high-converting ad for "Sculptra" in Dallas, we don't wait—we replicate that winning structure for similar med spas in Miami, Phoenix, and Denver immediately.
- **Procedure Economics**: We don't optimize for "leads." We optimize for the treatments that actually make the client money (e.g., prioritizing $3,000 Morpheus8 packages over $200 Botox units).
- **Automated Execution**: We use systems to generate ad copy, landing pages, and content at scale—not manual copywriting for every location.

## Core Problems Pulse Solves

### The Structural Problem: "Why Growth Breaks at Scale"

**The Complexity Threshold**: As organizations add locations, providers, and services, the complexity of managing thousands of keywords, creatives, and bids exceeds human capacity. Teams compensate by simplifying—using generic strategies that kill performance.

#### The Amnesia Trap
In traditional models, learning is isolated. If a campaign wins in Dallas, the team in Miami doesn't know. You pay for the same experiments over and over.

#### The Fragility Trap
Performance depends on manual effort. When a key employee leaves or a vendor changes, your "system" walks out the door, and execution resets.

**Pulse Solution**: Replace manual effort with learned infrastructure.

## Pulse Core Mechanism: "Learn Once. Apply Everywhere."

Pulse treats your entire network as a single laboratory.

### The Three-Step Process

1. **Detect** - The system observes a performance lift in Market A
2. **Validate** - It tests the variable against economic and competitive data
3. **Replicate** - Proven logic is instantly deployed to Markets B, C, and D

**The Result**: Your growth strategy improves while you sleep.

## Three Layers of Intelligence

### 1. Competitive Truth
> *"We see the board."*

Pulse doesn't guess where to bid; it knows. By integrating with Casa's market maps, Pulse sees exactly which competitors are active, where saturation is high, and where "opportunity gaps" exist in the local market. We deploy budget where your competitors are blind.

### 2. Performance Economics
> *"Volume is vanity."*

Pulse ignores "cheap clicks." It evaluates performance based on **Procedure Economics**. The system optimizes for the patients and services that generate actual margin and lifetime value, aligning acquisition velocity with operational capacity.

### 3. Behavioral Patterning
> *"Predicting the patient journey."*

Pulse analyzes conversion data across millions of interactions to understand the full funnel—from the first search to the third appointment. It recognizes the behavioral signals that indicate high-intent patients, allowing aggressive bidding on value, not just volume.

## Casa's Five Integrated Capability Layers

### Layer 1: Local Market Intelligence
Constructs and maintains structured representations of markets by vertical and geography. Markets are treated as living systems.

### Layer 2: Company & People Dossiers
Rich, structured dossiers for every company and person in the active market universe. Stateful and continuously updated.

**Company Dossiers Include**:
- Operating footprint and service areas
- Website structure, positioning, messaging
- Social presence and activity patterns
- Ratings, reviews, reputation signals
- Category focus, service mix, competitive context
- Historical outreach activity and response behavior

**People Dossiers Include**:
- Professional background and experience
- Education, training, certifications
- Functional responsibilities and decision influence
- Public presence and credibility signals
- Relationship to ownership, operations, growth

### Layer 3: Automated Personalization & Copy Generation
Deeply contextual outbound copy generated automatically using market, company, and people intelligence.

### Layer 4: Outbound Execution Engine
- Dynamic segment construction
- Sequencing and timing logic
- Deliverability-aware sending controls
- Reply handling and state classification

### Layer 5: Conversion Analytics & Optimization
Captures and analyzes real response signals. Learning is preserved and applied forward.
